Kadane's Algorithm

Estimated Time

1 hour

How the algorithm works ?

  • The algorithm iterates through the entire array, calculating the sum of subarrays ending at each index.
  • It keeps track of the maximum sum encountered during the traversal.
  • If the current subarray sum (sum) becomes negative, the algorithm discards it and starts with a new subarray from the next element by resetting sum to 0.
  • Bytheendof the loop, maximum contains the largest sum of any contiguous subarray in the array.
